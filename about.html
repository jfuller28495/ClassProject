Pets To Go

Description/Background

Founded in 2009, Pets To Go is a non-profit and Springfield’s only no-kill animal shelter. Since it’s opening Pets to Go has placed an estimated 18,000 homeless cats and dogs in new households. Pet’s To Go works in conjunction with the Animal Rescue League location services to locate the original owners of lost pets as well as finding new homes for abandoned animals. Pet’s To Go is relies on donations from United way, private donations, and volunteers to operate this facility.

Target Market/Customers

The majority of Pets To Go customers are an equal mix of family and single persons in the 25 to 40 year range.

Our Staff

Pets to Go has the most experienced staff in the tri-state area. We hire and staff veterinarians, veterinary technicians, animal welfare experts, and kennel attendants who have passed the highest levels of animal rights and animal welfare training. Each of our animals are carefully looked after by our team and are watched around-the-clock to ensure they're happy and healthy as they await their new homes. We also accept and train volunteers and students who are interested in helping the animals and learning more about the animal healthcare field.

What to Expect

Whether you're looking for a new pet or an exciting volunteer opportunity, Pets To Go has a lot to offer. Join our mailing list and we'll keep you informed about local events, volunteer opportunities, new adoptables, and recent alumni members who have shared their stories. Regardless of what you're looking for, our knowledgeable and experienced staff will help you find it, and help you learn more about animals and animal rights along the way.
